Scary Movie Gallery Dingbats Font (version 1)

2022 Iconian Fonts - Daniel Zadorozny

http://www.iconian.com


This is a dingbat font with over 70 silhouettes of classic horror movie logos plus several movie villain dingbats such as Freddy Krueger and Michael Myers.


Use of a character map program, such as Windows' Character Map, will be very helpful in exploring and using this font.
